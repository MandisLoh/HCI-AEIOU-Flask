Dear SUTD Family,

It has been 35 days since our team formally stepped up as the Cabinet of the 10th Student Government (ROOT) and the 6th Student Association Council (SAC). We are immensely grateful for the support thus far, and we hope that you can continue to trust us as we journey through the upcoming year together.

We are proud to present the 10th Cabinet Roadmap, an infographic to let all SUTDents know the plans, events and initiatives that we lay out for the 10th ROOT and 6th SAC. As the COVID-19 situation improves in Singapore, we hope to push towards the 3Rs mentioned in our campaign promise - Revitalise, Reallocate, and Reciprocate. Read more about the various initiatives and events in the roadmap!
